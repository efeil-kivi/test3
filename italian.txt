2, Gender of nouns
Every word has gender in Italian.

Like:
	Beautiful language.  language is feminine.
Masculine   -O
	Panino  sandwich 🥪
	Telefono  telephone 📞
	Libro  	book 📙
Feminine   -A
	Pasta  🥧
	Acqua water 💦
	Porta  door 🚪 
Other
	Cane  dog 🐶  ---- masculine 
	Carne  meat 🥩   ---- feminine
	Portone  main door 🚪  ---- masculine
	Pane  bread 🥪   ---- masculine 
Hard to tell  -E
	Fiume  river ---- masculine
	Informazione  information ---- feminine
	Chiave   key ---- feminine
	Canale  channel ---- masculine 
Words change because of gender.
Questa lavatrice e\ rotta.	---- feminine 
This washing machine is broken.

Quello stivale nero e\ bello.
That boot is beautiful.	---- masculine 

Quella nave e\ grandissima!	---- feminine 
That ship is huge.

3. Adjectives endings.
-O  masculine
	Bello 	rosso	buono	nero
-A  feminine
	Bella	rossa	buona 	nera
-E  both
	Interessante  interesting 
	Triste  sad 😔
4. Indefinite Articles
Un --- masculine 
	Un panino  a sandwich 🥪 
	Un portone  a main door 🚪 
	Un bagno  a bathroom 🚽
	
	Exception:  Un  uno ---- for speaking “strong sounds” also means “one.”
		 Uno  stranino   strange
		Uno gnomo  a gnome
		Uno zaino   a backpack 
		Uno xilofono   a xylophone
		Uno yogurt   a yogurt 
		Uno psicologo   a psychologist
Una --- feminine
	Una porta  a door 🚪 
	Una doccia  a shower 🚿 
	Una coperta  a blanket 
	
	Exception:  Una  un’   ---- for speaking
		Un’arancia  an orange 
		Un’insalata  a salad 🥗 
		Un’artista  an artist 🎨
